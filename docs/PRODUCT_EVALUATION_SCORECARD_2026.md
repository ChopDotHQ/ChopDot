## Detailed Scoring

## 1. Product thesis clarity — 9.0/10

Why high:
- shared commitment is a strong primitive
- the product is no longer wandering across too many identities
- the wedge and future expansion path now connect

What would move it to 10:
- repeated user evidence that the market also understands ChopDot this way

## 2. Wedge strength — 8.5/10

Why high:
- group deposits are urgent
- multi-party coordination pain is real
- deadlines and social risk make the wedge legible

What keeps it from 10:
- not yet validated enough with real recurring users

## 3. UX/product fit direction — 7.5/10

Why strong:
- the UI/UX translation is now clear
- the app can stay in its current shell while changing meaning

- proof that users understand the state machine and closure semantics in practice

## 4. Technical architecture — 8.0/10

Why strong:

## 5. API readiness posture — 8.0/10

Why strong:

## 6. Security / trust posture — 7.0/10

## 12. Investor readiness — 7.5/10

ChopDot is now:
- far more serious

But it is not yet:
- battle-proven
- retention-proven
- pricing-proven

## What improves the score fastest

### Biggest upside moves

1. restore the chapter / closeout loop cleanly
2. validate real organizer/community usage
3. prove repeat usage
4. prove willingness-to-pay
5. tighten the legal/operational frame

## What would hurt the score

- drifting back into generic expense-app framing
- reintroducing complexity before the wedge is proven
- blurring coordination-first posture into custody-like behavior
- leading with builder/API or token narratives before product proof

## Best One-Line Summary

**ChopDot currently scores as a strong strategic product thesis with credible architecture and monetization logic, but still needs product proof, repeat usage, and pricing evidence before it can score like a truly validated business.**
