# Big Dog Readiness

Use this when thinking about:

- serious investors
- strategic infra players
- the difference between “interesting” and “hard to dismiss”

Detailed source:

- [Big Dog Readiness Matrix](../docs/BIG_DOG_READINESS_MATRIX.md)

## Current read

- category clarity: stronger
- architecture direction: stronger
- monetization logic: stronger
- proof, traction, retention, pricing validation: still the biggest gaps

## Rule

The real big dogs will care less about the story alone and more about:

- repeat usage
- willingness-to-pay
- trust posture
- legal discipline
- platform potential
