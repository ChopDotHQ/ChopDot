# Finance And Monetization

## Today

- primary model: subscriptions
- buyer: organizer, community admin, SMB/provider operator
- participant remains free in the base case
- no reliance on custody spread, yield, or token economics

## Tomorrow

- stronger subscriptions
- premium trust / proof actions
- builder/API pricing
- provider workflow pricing

## Finance status

- infra cost is not the main problem
- people cost and operating shape are the main problem
- the current base case is not self-sustaining by year 5
